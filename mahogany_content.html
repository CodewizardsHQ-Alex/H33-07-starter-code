Mahogany

Mahogany tree is a large, semi-evergreen tree with a canopy that casts dappled shade.

It is a popular landscape tree in southern Florida and is the national tree of the Dominican Republic and Belize. Mahogany is a valuable lumber used for paneling, furniture, boats, musical instruments, and other items. Honduran mahogany is the most widespread and the only genuine mahogany species commercially grown today.
